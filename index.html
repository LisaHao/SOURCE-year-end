2016 Year in Review

Letter From The Managers

We are thrilled to present our 2014-2015 Year-End Report to you, our friends and supporters. We started this year with a commitment to connect SOURCE’s work to the organization’s core values. We hope that in reading this report, you can see SOURCE’s values coming through in our work and our impact on the community. This report contains highlights from our commu-nity partnerships and our efforts to develop our incredible students.

This year boasted a variety of new initiatives, including the SOURCE Summit, SOURCE Pathways News-letter, the Associate Consultant pro-gram, and Nonprofit Brown Bags. Our teams created lasting impact on the community through their partner-ships with our Clients; for example, our team working with the Career and Technical Education Centers at Fairplex created a comprehensive Financial Literacy Handbook that will be widely distributed to help high school students understand how to manage their finances. In total, our work with Client partnerships directly involved 22 community leaders, who valued our team’s work at over $26,450.

In 2014-2015, our nonprofit Clients touched the lives of over 56,000 individuals in Claremont and nationally. Through our broader engagement efforts, we engaged with over 35 local organizations and 118 nonprofit leaders in Claremont.

Without the passion and dedication of this year’s 33 SOURCE students, none of this impact would be possible. SOURCE students spent over 400 hours supporting and growing local nonprofits. Our team dedicated themselves to bringing value to their Client partnerships, as well as implementing new initia-tives, such as creating content for our Associate Consultant program or brainstorming new ways to engage donors.. As we wrap up the year, we wish our graduating seniors the best of luck moving forward, we look ahead to another exciting year with the Claremont Community.

Our Mission

The mission of SOURCE is to help local nonprofits build capacity and increase efficiency by providing professional consulting services at zero cost to the organizations, while simultaneously enhancing student development.
